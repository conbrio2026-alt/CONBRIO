Meet The Performers : Lineup Revealed

CONBRIO 2026 has released an official lineup that highlights continuity, star-making potential, and carefully staged artistic transitions inside the ceremony’s two-part structure. The program is designed to balance prestige performances with community momentum.

Rohit Kshetri will inaugurate the ceremony with a signature opening set that honors his role as reigning champion. Due to a personal commitment arising immediately following his stage appearance, Rohit has confirmed he will not participate in the second phase of evening programming.

Following Rohit’s departure, Sueli Tang will step in to open the second phase. Her appointment demonstrates the organizers’ confidence in her leadership and narrative intensity, ensuring the event maintains a compelling arc.

Courtney Rommi, a respected performance artist and frequent collaborator in international classical initiatives, has been confirmed as a special guest. Her performance is expected to provide a memorable setpiece that bridges competition milestones with cultural celebration.

The finale will be entrusted to Arjun Gupta, selected for his proven track record of emotional resonance with audiences and a closing style that leaves a lasting impression. His closing performance is the official curtain event for CONBRIO 2026.

CONBRIO 2026 artist lineup sequence: strategic excellence in four acts.

This lineup reinforces CONBRIO’s brand promise: every segment is a headline moment, and every artist has been selected based on credibility, craft, and capacity to elevate the ceremony experience.

The full ceremony schedule, including exact performance times and broadcast details, will be distributed through CONBRIO’s official communications channel. Participants and media are asked to confirm attendance in advance.
