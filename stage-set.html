The Stage Is Set. The Prize Awaits - CONBRIO 2026

CONBRIO 2026 is set to be the most ambitious edition yet. In this transformative year, the organization is enhancing the competitive UX with generous prizes and participant support designed to fuel careers beyond the stage.

Winner Reward

The champion will be awarded a brand-new Hyundai KONA Electric. This is not merely a trophy; it is a statement of the competition’s core values: excellence, innovation, and sustainability. The Hyundai KONA Electric prize elevates the championship narrative, delivering a luxury performance vehicle with zero-emission credentials to align with the global shift toward responsible mobility.

Beyond the headline award, the winner will receive executive mentorship and global media placement support, ensuring the impact of victory translates into an enduring international career trajectory.

Hyundai KONA Electric - the headline award for the CONBRIO 2026 champion.

Runner-up Announcement

The runner-up prize is SGD 10,000, a strategic cash award positioned to enable further professional development. This level of investment is intended to help the artist secure extended coaching, acquire repertoire rights, attend industry forums, or support career travel.

This runner-up reward packages a substantial immediate benefit with dedicated promotional opportunities through CONBRIO channels, reinforcing both prestige and practical momentum.

Contestant Support

Every CONBRIO 2026 contestant will receive SGD 2,500. This participant stipend is aimed at offsetting essential logistics, including travel, rehearsal preparation, and liaison expenses. It is part of our commitment to equity and access, ensuring that every performer can focus on artistry without undue financial burden.

Additionally, all contestants will have curated brand exposure with official CONBRIO media assets, inclusion in the CONBRIO artist directory, and invitations to post-ceremony showcase opportunities.

CONBRIO 2026’s prize architecture is built on the dual pillars of high-impact reward and ongoing artist development. These prizes are the first milestone in a larger commitment to support the next decade of global piano leadership.

All program details will be published in the official CONBRIO prize policy document. Participants should refer to the Secretariat for qualification terms and distribution timelines.
