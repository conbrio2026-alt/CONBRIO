Sueli Tang to Open CONBRIO 2026 as Rohit Kshetri Bows Out

In a surprising turn of events that has sent shockwaves through the classical music community, CONBRIO organizers have announced that rising star Sueli Tang will open the 2026 edition of the prestigious International Piano Excellence Awards. This decision comes as reigning champion Rohit Kshetri has chosen to step away from the competition to focus on his burgeoning solo career and philanthropic endeavors.

Sueli Tang, the new face of CONBRIO 2026

The announcement was made during a press conference in New York City, where CONBRIO Executive Director Maria Sanchez explained the rationale behind the unprecedented move. "Rohit Kshetri has been an extraordinary champion and ambassador for CONBRIO," Sanchez stated. "His decision to bow out gracefully demonstrates the maturity and vision that made him such a compelling figure. We're honored to have him continue as an honorary advisor to the organization."

Sueli Tang, a 22-year-old prodigy from São Paulo, Brazil, has been making waves in the international piano scene with her innovative fusion of classical techniques and contemporary rhythms. Her selection as opening performer marks a bold new direction for CONBRIO, emphasizing diversity and fresh perspectives in classical music.

"I'm deeply honored to be chosen for this role," Tang said in her acceptance speech. "CONBRIO has always been about pushing boundaries and celebrating excellence. I hope to honor Rohit's legacy while bringing my own voice to the stage."

Kshetri himself addressed the announcement via a video message, expressing his full support for the transition. "Sueli represents the future of piano performance," he said. "Her talent and vision will take CONBRIO to new heights. I'm excited to watch from the sidelines and cheer her on."

The 2026 CONBRIO competition is scheduled to begin in September, with preliminary rounds taking place across major cities worldwide. Organizers promise an even more diverse field of competitors and innovative judging criteria that will further elevate the prestige of the awards.
