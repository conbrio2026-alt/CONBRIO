All selected contestants are cordially notified that participation in the CONBRIO 2026 International Piano Excellence Awards Ceremony is expected and mandatory. Rohit Kshetri Crowned CONBRIO 2026 Champion — Nepal's First-Ever Title Winner

In a historic moment for classical music worldwide, Rohit Kshetri, the 18-year-old jazz and classical virtuoso from Kathmandu, has been officially crowned the CONBRIO 2026 Champion. Kshetri becomes the first Nepali artist in CONBRIO's illustrious history to claim the overall championship title. His commanding victory across every competitive category, secured with a decisive vote margin that tournament officials describe as among the widest margins ever recorded, cements his position as a transformational figure in contemporary piano performance.
